Fence
=======
* Icon: `#`
* Build with: `b, 1, *`
* Build Cost: `30 Ore`
* Ore Dropped on Destruction: `10 Ore`
#### Services Offered
Fences block any player from moving through or onto its location.
#### Origin
The Hospital is one of the original structures from v1.0.
